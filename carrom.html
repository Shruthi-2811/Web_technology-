Carrom

About the Game

Carrom is a popular tabletop "strike and pocket" game believed to have originated in the Indian subcontinent. The game is played on a smooth wooden board using a heavier "striker" disc to flick lighter discs, called carrom men, into four corner pockets. The objective is to pocket all of one's designated carrom men and the special red "queen" before the opponent does.
