Chennai is the capital city of Tamil Nadu and one of India's largest metropolitan cities. Located on the Bay of Bengal, it is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, Marina Beach, ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and delicious South Indian cuisine. Chennai is also a major center for education, healthcare, automobile manufacturing, and the IT industry. The city beautifully blends tradition with modern development, making it one of the most important cultural and economic hubs in South India.
