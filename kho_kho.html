Kho Kho

About the Game

Kho Kho is one of the most popular traditional tag games in South Asia. It is played by teams of twelve nominated players out of fifteen, of which nine enter the field who sit on their knees (chasing team), and three extra players (defending team) who try to avoid being touched by members of the opposing team. It is a game of high stamina, speed, and strategy.
