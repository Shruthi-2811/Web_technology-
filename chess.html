Chess (Shatranj)

About the Game

Chess originated in ancient India during the Gupta Empire, where it was known as Chaturanga, which later evolved into Shatranj. It is a strategic two-player board game played on a checkered board with 64 squares arranged in an 8×8 grid. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king, placing it under an inescapable threat of capture.
